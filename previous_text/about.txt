ABOUT STEWART CHANG
Independent consultant in global health modeling with a passion for translating complex data into actionable insights for better health outcomes worldwide.

MY JOURNEY TO CONSULTING
My path to global health modeling began with dual interests in mathematics and biology, leading me from Rice University to the University of Michigan for doctoral studies in bioinformatics. Early career research at the University of British Columbia and University of Washington grounded me in computational biology and systems thinking.

​

The opportunity to join the Gates Foundation's Institute for Disease Modeling in 2012 was transformative – it allowed me to apply mathematical rigor to some of the world's most pressing health challenges. After 10+ years of increasingly senior roles, I recognized the need for independent modeling expertise that could serve the broader global health community without institutional constraints.

​

Model Stewardship represents my commitment to bringing the same analytical rigor and collaborative approach that served major foundation initiatives to organizations of all sizes seeking evidence-based solutions.
